Services

A Complete Printing Solution Under One Roof

From design to delivery, Kamala Press Pvt. Ltd. handles every stage of the print process in-house - offset and digital presses, pre-press platemaking, lamination, spot UV, foiling and laser cutting. Pick the service you need below for details.

Offset Printing

Heidelberg SM-74 four- and five-colour offset presses for sharp, consistent, high-volume commercial print work.

Digital Printing

Short runs, fast turnarounds and personalised printing - perfect for proofs, business cards, events and variable-data jobs.

Printing & Designing

In-house design and pre-press: Adobe artwork, CTP platemaking and Heidelberg Prepress Interface - your idea, press-ready.

Lamination

Thermal, BOPP and PVC lamination in gloss or matte to protect printed work and add a premium, durable finish.

Spot UV & Foiling

High-gloss spot UV and metallic foil stamping to draw the eye and lift premium invitations, business cards and packaging.

Laser Cutting

Precise laser cutting for intricate cards, packaging windows, decorative cut-outs and short-run jobs without custom dies.
