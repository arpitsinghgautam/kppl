Laser Cutting

Some jobs need cuts a die simply cannot make - intricate filigree on a wedding card, a precise window in a packaging carton, an unusual outline that would dull a steel rule after a hundred sheets. Laser cutting solves that. Our laser system cuts clean, accurate edges on paper, board, and selected synthetic substrates without the cost or lead time of custom dies.

Because there is no physical die, design changes are cheap and prototypes are fast. That makes laser cutting ideal for short runs, samples, and intricate decorative work.

Common laser-cut applications

  • Wedding invitations and laser-cut greeting cards
  • Decorative packaging, gift boxes and sleeves with windows
  • Custom labels, tags and bookmarks
  • Signage, name plates and promotional cut-outs
  • Architectural and design-school models

Share your vector artwork and the substrate you have in mind, and we will advise on the minimum line weight, kerf and burn-edge characteristics so the finished piece looks exactly the way it does on screen.
