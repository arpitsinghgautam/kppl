Digital Printing

Digital printing is the answer when you need it fast, in short runs, or personalised. There are no plates to make and no make-ready waste, so a job of 50 brochures costs roughly the same per sheet as a job of 500. That makes it ideal for prototypes, last-minute reprints, event collateral, and any project where data changes between copies.

Over the last few years KPPL has invested heavily in digital production: a Canon ImagePress C9010 VP arrived in 2022, followed by a Ricoh Pro C7200X, and most recently a Ricoh Pro C9500 in November 2024. Between them, that's photo-quality digital output on sheets up to 49.9 inches, on stocks up to 470 gsm, at 115 sheets per minute — production-grade output without the offset lead time.

Best for

  • Business cards, postcards and personalised mailers
  • Short-run brochures, flyers and booklets
  • Event invitations, wedding cards and announcements
  • Sample books, dummies and proofs before offset runs
  • Variable-data jobs: numbered tickets, name tags, certificates
  • Short-run commercial and packaging jobs on heavier stocks

Turnaround on most digital jobs is 24 to 48 hours. Drop us your file - we'll handle paper selection, finishing and delivery.

Machinery Used for This Service

The presses and post-press equipment our team runs to deliver this service.

Ricoh Pro C9500

Our newest addition (November 2024) — flagship Ricoh production press for short-run commercial and packaging work on heavier stocks.

  • Sheets up to 49.9"
  • Stocks up to 470 gsm
  • 115 ppm (upgradable to 135)
  • Industry-leading resolution

Ricoh Pro C7200X

Our first Ricoh production engine — workhorse for everyday digital colour work alongside the Canon. Switching to Ricoh in 2023 was a deliberate move to improve productivity on short-run jobs.

  • Production-grade digital colour
  • Coated, uncoated and textured stocks
  • 5th-colour station for spot effects

Canon ImagePress C9010 VP

Installed 2022 to handle increased demand for short-run commercial and packaging jobs. Reliable workhorse that still does a lot of our everyday digital output.

  • Canon ImagePress production engine
  • Photo-quality CMYK
  • Short-run commercial & packaging
