Offset Printing

Offset printing remains the gold standard for high-volume, high-quality commercial print work. At Kamala Press Pvt. Ltd. we run brand-new Heidelberg Speedmaster SM-74 four-colour and five-colour machines with auto-plate changers, giving you sharp registration, consistent colour, and the flexibility to print on a wide range of paper stocks and finishes.

Whether you need a thousand catalogues or a hundred thousand brochures, offset gives you the best price-per-unit for medium-to-large runs without compromising on the punch and fidelity of every sheet. Our pre-press studio is equipped with a Technova CTP plate processor and a Heidelberg Prepress Interface, so the file you approve is the file we print.

What we print on offset

  • Brochures, catalogues, leaflets and folders
  • Posters, calendars and wobblers
  • Corporate stationery: letterheads, envelopes, visiting cards
  • Newsletters, menu cards and greeting cards
  • Packaging cartons and utility boxes

Need help choosing the right stock, finish, or colour profile for your job? Our team has been doing this since 1990 - send us your file or a brief and we will recommend the cleanest, most cost-effective way to get it printed.
