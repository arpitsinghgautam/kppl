Printing & Designing

A great print job starts long before the press runs. Our in-house design and pre-press team uses the latest Adobe Creative suite on HP Proliant workstations to take your idea from rough brief to print-ready artwork - and then through our Technova CTP platemaking and Heidelberg Prepress Interface, straight to the press.

If you already have a design, we will check it, fix bleeds and colour profiles, and proof it before plates are burned. If you don't, we will design it for you from scratch: logo, layout, typography, the whole thing.

Design services we offer

  • Logo and brand identity development
  • Brochure, catalogue and newsletter layout
  • Packaging design and dieline creation
  • Business cards, letterheads and corporate stationery
  • Greeting cards, calendars and invitations
  • Pre-press: file prep, colour management, CTP platemaking

Working with one shop for design and printing means fewer surprises, faster proof cycles, and a finished piece that looks the way you intended.

Technova VioStar - Violet Photopolymer Plates

India's first and the world's third violet photopolymer digital plate. Manufactured by Technova in collaboration with Agfa Gevaert. Excellent dot reproduction, low dot gain, long run-length, and compatibility with UV inks.

  • Plate type: negative-working violet laser
  • Sensitivity: 405 nm violet diode
  • Image colour: blue
  • Run-length: up to 400,000 impressions (unbaked)
  • Gauge: 0.15 / 0.20 / 0.28 mm
