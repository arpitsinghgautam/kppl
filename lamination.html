Lamination

Lamination protects your printed work and gives it a finished, premium feel. We offer thermal lamination as well as BOPP and PVC films, in both gloss and matte, on sheet sizes up to the full Heidelberg SM-74 format.

The right film does more than just seal a sheet - it deepens colour, resists smudges and moisture, and gives book covers, menu cards and packaging the durability they need to survive everyday handling.

What we laminate

  • Book covers, magazine covers and presentation folders
  • Menu cards, table tents and restaurant collateral
  • Packaging cartons, gift boxes and product sleeves
  • Posters, ID cards and visiting cards
  • Calendars, certificates and corporate gifting

Not sure whether gloss or matte is right for your job? Send us a sample of the stock and we will recommend the finish that holds the colour best and feels right in the hand.
