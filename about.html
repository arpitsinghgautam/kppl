About Us

Company Profile

Kamala Press Pvt. Ltd. was founded by Shri Anjani Kumar Singh in 1990 as a proprietorship firm and has since grown into one of the most trusted names in commercial printing in Varanasi and the wider eastern Uttar Pradesh region. Through three decades of constant change in media, market demand and technology, the company has adapted and grown without compromising on the values it was founded on - high-quality printing, dependable customer service, and steady investment in state-of-the-art equipment.

Today we serve elite corporate organisations, publishers, advertising agencies and businesses of every size, helping them sharpen their brand image and produce work that holds up next to anything coming out of the country's biggest presses.

Idea

Whether you are a small, medium, or established business, Kamala Press Pvt. Ltd. offers creative and customised printing & packaging solutions for your visual identity and packaging needs - a complete printing solution under one roof.

Vision

Empower businesses of all sizes with high-quality, sensibly priced printing and packaging solutions that enhance their visual identity and protect their products. We aim to be the trusted long-term partner that delivers creative work which meets each client's unique needs and exceeds their expectations.
