Spot UV & Foiling

Spot UV and foiling are the finishes that take a printed piece from good to memorable. Spot UV is a high-gloss varnish applied only to specific areas of the design - a logo, a headline, a pattern - so it catches the light against a matte background. Foiling stamps metallic gold, silver or coloured foil onto the sheet for a luxurious, tactile accent.

Together, they give business cards, wedding invitations, packaging and premium brochures a finish that simply cannot be reproduced digitally. Used well, a single spot of UV or foil draws the eye exactly where you want it to go.

Where these finishes shine

  • Wedding invitations and high-end greeting cards
  • Premium business cards and corporate stationery
  • Luxury packaging, gift boxes and product cartons
  • Annual reports, brochures and presentation folders
  • Certificates, awards and limited-edition print runs

Send us your artwork with the spot-UV or foil areas marked on a separate layer, and we will produce a proof before going to plate.
